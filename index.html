Services

Work with us.

Available as a freelance engagement through ZynthoLab, or as a full-time hire. The work is the same. The channel is different.

Service 01

Consultancy

Strategic advice on lab operations and science communication. ZynthoLab assesses your current workflow, identifies the friction points that are costing your team time, and delivers a clear, actionable roadmap.

What's included

  • — Current workflow audit and gap analysis
  • — Lab operations and admin review
  • — Science communication infrastructure assessment
  • — Prioritised recommendations and roadmap
  • — Follow-up advisory session

Service 02

Custom Workflow Design

Bespoke system builds tailored to your lab environment. ZynthoLab designs and builds the operational infrastructure your team needs — from Notion-based lab management systems to AI automations and science communication frameworks.

What's included

  • — Notion-based lab management system build
  • — Inventory, compliance, and reporting systems
  • — AI automation setup for research admin
  • — Science writing and communication frameworks
  • — Presentation and infographic design

Service 03

Set Up and Training

Full implementation of designed workflows and hands-on training so your team can use, maintain, and build on what has been built — without depending on outside help to keep it running.

What's included

  • — System set up and configuration
  • — Live onboarding session for research teams
  • — Written documentation and process guides
  • — Team training on day-to-day use
  • — Post-launch support period
